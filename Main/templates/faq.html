Buying Vehicles

Questions about purchasing vehicles

You can search vehicles using:

  • Basic search by make or model
  • Advanced filters (price, year, mileage)
  • Vehicle type and location
  • Fuel type and transmission

No registration required for searching and browsing.

To contact a seller:

  1. Register as a Customer (free)
  2. Login to your account
  3. Find a vehicle you're interested in
  4. Click "Contact Seller" button
  5. Connect via WhatsApp directly

Yes! Registered customers can save vehicles to favorites.

This helps you:

  • Compare different vehicles
  • Keep track of options
  • Quickly contact sellers

Selling Vehicles

Questions about selling your vehicle

To create a listing:

  1. Register as a Seller
  2. Login to your account
  3. Click "Create Listing"
  4. Fill in vehicle details
  5. Submit for admin approval

Listing approval typically takes less than 24 hours.

Your listing will be reviewed by our admin team before becoming visible to buyers.

Yes! As a Seller, you have full control:

  • Update vehicle details
  • Change prices
  • Modify descriptions
  • Mark as sold
  • Delete listings

Renting Vehicles

Questions about vehicle rentals

Yes! You can list your vehicle for rent, sale, or both.

When creating a listing, specify:

  • Rental price and terms
  • Availability dates
  • Rental duration options

Insurance is important for rentals.

Always discuss and verify insurance requirements directly with the other party before finalizing any rental agreement.
